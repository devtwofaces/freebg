ABOUT US

ISKCON Brampton (also known as Vedic Education Centre) is located at 173 Advance Blvd Unit 41, Brampton, ON L6T 4Z7 Canada. It offers a wide array of learning opportunities for those interested in learning about Vedic studies. The centre also offers classes in yoga, vegetarian cooking, dance, health and well-being for both children and adults. On Sundays, the centre, which is open to the public offers free vegetarian feasts, along with music and Vedic discourses by ISKCON guest speakers. There is also a Sunday school for children aged 5-14 years.
